Behavioral questions

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. What are your strengths / weaknesses?
  3. Why should we hire you?
  4. Tell me about a challenging interaction with a teammate.
  5. How do you handle pressure?
  6. What is your favorite website?
  7. What do you like the most and least about working in this industry?
  8. What questions do you have for me?

Base Knowledge questions (technical)

  1. What is clean code?
  2. What development tools have you used?
  3. What source control tools have you used?
  4. Tell me about the project you are most proud of, and what your contribution was.
  5. What is MVC?
  6. What is OO? - give an example
  7. What is an object?
  8. What is a class?
  9. What is the relationship between an object and a class?
  10. What is the difference between arrays and collection?
  11. What is the difference between a symbol and a string?
